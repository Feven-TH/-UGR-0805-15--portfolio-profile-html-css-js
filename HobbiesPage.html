My Hobbies

Spiritual Service

Spiritual service is an important part of my life. I’m involved in church activities like group prayers, Bible studies, and discussions, and I’m always learning through the courses I take at church. Serving others in my community has been a rewarding experience, and it’s helped me strengthen my faith while building deeper connections with those around me.

Books

Reading has always been a way for me to escape into new worlds and expand my mind. Whether it’s a gripping novel, an insightful history book, or a spiritual writing, I love how books can transport me and offer new perspectives. Some authors have really stuck with me, shaping how I see the world.

Movies

Watching movies is one of my favorite pastimes. I enjoy a mix of genres, but I especially love thrillers, mystery, thought-provoking themes, or moments of joy and laughter. Classic Hollywood films and early 2000s movies often captivate me the most.

Discussions

I enjoy meaningful discussions on societal, cultural, spiritual, political, and historical topics. Debating ideas challenges me to think critically and deepen my understanding of the world. Listening to podcasts sparks new ideas and broadens my knowledge. However, it’s the conversations and debates that truly energize and help me grow.
