Interests And Adventures

The world is a book, and those who do not travel read only one page.

— Saint Augustine

Wenchi

My family and I visited Wenchi Crater Lake, and it was an unforgettable experience. The stunning views of the lush landscape & the peaceful waters of the lake made it a perfect getaway. Exploring the area together made the trip even more special, creating lasting memories. It was a beautiful moment of connection, both with nature & each other.

Suba

Suba was such a great hiking trip, a perfect way to get away from the noise of the city and just be surrounded by nature. Walking through the forest felt so refreshing, and it was the kind of peace I really needed. The hike up Damotcha Mountain was challenging, but reaching the summit amidst the clouds was an incredible feeling. Standing there, I felt so proud of myself, especially when I checked my phone and saw I had walked about 23.41 km and taken 37,757 steps. It was a rewarding and memorable adventure!

Reflection

Pilgrimages like my visit to Etisa Abune Teklehaymanot are unforgettable. In the dark cave, "አምሳለ ሲዖል" fear took over, but the tiny light at the exit brought hope and reminded me of God’s mercy. These journeys are my escape, a connection to the saints, and a source of inner peace.
